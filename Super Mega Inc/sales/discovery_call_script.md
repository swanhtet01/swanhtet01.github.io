# Discovery Call Script (30 Minutes)

## 0-5 min: Objective lock

- What single business outcome matters most in the next 60 days?
- What happens if this is not solved?

## 5-12 min: Current process map

- Which steps are manual?
- Which systems and files are involved?
- Who owns each step today?

## 12-20 min: Pain and risk quantification

- Where are delays, errors, or handoff failures happening?
- What are the recurring decision bottlenecks?
- Which issues are highest business impact?

## 20-26 min: Scope shaping

- Which package best fits current maturity?
- What data access is available now?
- What can be delivered in first sprint?

## 26-30 min: Close and next action

- Confirm target outcomes and scope boundary.
- Confirm proposal turnaround (<24h).
- Confirm stakeholders for sign-off.
