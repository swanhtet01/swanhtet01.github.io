# Lead Qualification Checklist

## Must-have criteria

- Clear business owner identified
- Defined problem with measurable impact
- Data access available (email/files/sheets/system exports)
- Decision window within 90 days
- Budget range aligned with Starter/Growth/Scale

## Scoring

- Business urgency: Low / Medium / High
- Data readiness: Low / Medium / High
- Operational fit: Low / Medium / High
- Stakeholder commitment: Low / Medium / High

## Routing decision

- If all must-have criteria are yes: move to `Qualified`
- If one missing and recoverable: hold in `New Lead` with follow-up
- If multiple missing: close as `Lost - Not Ready`
