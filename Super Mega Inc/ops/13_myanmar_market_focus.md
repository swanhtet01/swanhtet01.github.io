# Myanmar Market Focus

## First ICP
- owner-led distributors
- importers
- warehouses
- light factories
- service operators

## Why this market first
- work is still scattered across Facebook, Viber, WhatsApp, Gmail, Excel, and paper
- founders and managers still approve too much manually
- missed follow-up and unclear ownership cost money quickly

## The first 3 offers

### Sales Follow-up Desk
- use for: leads, quotes, callbacks, follow-up
- outcome: one list, one next step, one owner

### Receiving Control
- use for: shortages, holds, GRN gaps, inbound problems
- outcome: log once, assign owner, stop losing exceptions

### Founder Daily Brief
- use for: owner or GM daily control
- outcome: one short daily view of what needs attention

## The next 2 offers

### Approval Flow
- use for: purchase approvals, exception sign-off, expense review
- outcome: one approval queue, one decision trail, less verbal confusion

### Order Inbox Desk
- use for: inbound orders from Facebook, Viber, WhatsApp, phone, and counter staff
- outcome: one order queue, one owner, one visible next action

## Wording rule

Use:
- simple systems
- follow-up
- receiving
- daily control
- one queue
- next step

Avoid:
- ERP
- autonomous agents
- SaaS replacement
- digital transformation
- AI-native platform

- Dispatch / Delivery Desk
- Service Job Desk
