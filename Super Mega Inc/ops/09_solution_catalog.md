# Solution Catalog

## Sales Desk
- Buyer: owner-led distributors, importers, and commercial teams
- Promise: keep prospecting, list cleanup, outreach, and follow-up in one system
- First rollout: sales queue plus founder brief

## Receiving Control
- Buyer: warehouse, plant, and procurement teams
- Promise: keep GRN gaps, holds, variances, and supplier follow-up visible
- First rollout: receiving queue plus manager review

## Founder Daily Brief
- Buyer: founders, GMs, and directors
- Promise: one short daily view of what needs attention
- First rollout: brief driven from live queues and exceptions

## Client Portal
- Buyer: service firms, project teams, and B2B operators
- Promise: one place for status, approvals, files, and follow-up
- First rollout: status board plus approval flow

## Learning Hub
- Buyer: companies rolling out onboarding, SOPs, and training
- Promise: guided learning with AI help and a simple operating view
- First rollout: one training path plus completion tracking
