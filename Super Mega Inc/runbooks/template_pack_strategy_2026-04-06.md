## SuperMega template pack strategy

### Public proof tools

- Find Companies
- Company List
- Receiving Log

These stay simple, free to try, and usable with any team's own data.

### Paid setup templates

#### 1. Sales Setup

- uses: Find Companies + Company List
- buyer: founders, sales teams, distributor owners
- outcome: one shortlist, one follow-up list, one repeatable outreach loop

#### 2. Company Cleanup

- uses: Company List + Task List
- buyer: teams already sitting on spreadsheets, CRM exports, scraped lists
- outcome: one clean working list, stage labels, next-step tasks

#### 3. Receiving Control

- uses: Receiving Log + Task List
- buyer: plants, stores, procurement, inbound teams
- outcome: one short issue log and one owned follow-up queue

### Private/internal templates

#### Yangon Tyre Plant A

- keep as a private tenant preset and rollout example
- use as a reference operating template for receiving + task follow-up
- do not let it shape the public website

#### Founder Brief

- add-on on top of any template
- one short daily review of leads, tasks, issues, and approvals

### Durable agent loops

- Revenue Scout
  reruns saved searches and adds fresh companies
- List Clerk
  cleans imported rows and keeps lists usable
- Task Triage
  turns messy notes into short next-step tasks
- Founder Brief
  summarizes the current operating state

### Sales rule

- do not pitch a platform
- do not pitch six products
- pitch one template that matches the client's current mess

### Current ladder

1. free proof tool
2. paid setup
3. add-on brief or automation
4. private tenant rollout only after the first loop works
