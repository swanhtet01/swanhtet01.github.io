# SuperMega Product Ladder

## Public Free Tools

1. Find Companies
   Search a market, keep the shortlist, and create the first follow-up.

2. Company List
   Paste or upload an existing company list and turn it into a usable working list.

3. Receiving Log
   Paste inbound shortages, holds, missing documents, or quantity issues and keep one clear follow-up list.

## Paid Setups

1. Sales Setup
   Use Find Companies plus Company List to create one live prospecting and outreach loop.

2. Company Cleanup
   Clean one imported list, tag it, stage it, and create the first next-step tasks.

3. Receiving Control
   Run Receiving Log for one site, warehouse, or plant team and keep the issue queue visible.

## Internal Agent Loops

1. Revenue Scout
   Find and qualify companies, then propose the next outreach action.

2. List Clerk
   Clean imported company lists, normalize fields, and flag gaps.

3. Task Triage
   Turn updates, blockers, and receiving issues into short task lists.

4. Founder Brief
   Summarize the real operating state from shared leads, tasks, and exceptions.

## What SuperMega Is Not

- not a broad AI platform
- not a generic ERP replacement
- not a self-serve product suite for every industry
- not a browser agent company first

## Current Company Thesis

SuperMega should sell small tools that clean messy business inputs into usable working lists. The wedge is immediate clarity, not deep system replacement.

## Rollout Order

1. Keep `supermega.dev` focused on the three free tools.
2. Keep `app.supermega.dev` as the shared team workspace and agent runtime.
3. Land the first real users on one paid setup at a time.
4. Expand only after one list or one issue flow is running well.
